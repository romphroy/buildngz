From the business

Petershan Coffee is the East London cafe bringing people together by tackling homelessness one espresso at a time. At Second Shot you can get incredible coffee, great food, and a unique atmosphere, and we just so happen to be changing lives.

We employ people who have been affected by homelessness, train them as top quality baristas, before transitioning them on to past or preferred employment. We're committed to taking our staff from where they are to where they deserve to be.
